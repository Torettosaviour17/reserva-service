Additional information

  • Our hall opens from 8.00am to 6.00pm. To secure a firm booking, a minimum of 50% non-refundable cost of the hall must be paid one week from the date of booking, and the balance payable one month after the initial payment. Where the event is less than two months from the date of booking, full payment must be made at the time of booking.

  • There is minimum refundable deposit to be paid at the time of booking as caution fee. The deposit will be retained until after event and when the premises have been inspected by the Facility Manager. The deposit will be returned if hall is free of damage and no theft occurred.

  • Period of hire covers 8 hours per day. Extra hour attracts a minimum charge per hour up to a maximum of 2 hours depending on the hall in use

  • Projector and Screen is for rent. Use of Sound Systems (Speakers Microphone etc. attracts a charge)

  • Clients must promptly evacuate all sets and stage properties immediately after their events. Failure to do so will attract a surcharge per day. Our management team reserves the right to dispose off sets not collected within 7 days.
